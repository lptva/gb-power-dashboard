Utilisation ranking Observed Proxy / Derived

Cables ranked by how often flow ran near a practical limit in the selected range. GB publishes no per-cable limits and no flow-based shadow prices: the working ceiling per direction is the highest flow sustained ≥2 h over the trailing 90 days (Proxy — self-adjusts to de-ratings, and single-period metering spikes don't set it), with operator nameplate as cited reference context only. Near-capacity = |flow| ≥ 90% of that ceiling. Δ compares GB MID with the counterparty day-ahead price (Derived £, indicative only — different market segments). Moyle, East-West and Greenlink share the all-island SEM day-ahead series; each row's Δ is averaged over its own near-capacity periods, so the three still differ. The congestion proxy column flags half-hours at ceiling and with a wide spread in the direction the flow earns — an approximation, not a shadow price (GB's cables are explicitly allocated; no congestion rent is published). Wide spread with slack flow, and at-ceiling flow against the price signal, are deliberately not flagged. ⓘ

Counterparty context Observed Derived £

What was happening in the zone on the other end of the selected cable: the cable's flow (GB side), GB price against the zone's day-ahead price — converted to £ at the daily BoE EUR/GBP rate and indicative only (day-ahead auction vs within-day MID, different market segments) — and the zone's generation mix below.
